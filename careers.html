Teacher Vacancies

Dream's School is looking for dedicated and enthusiastic teachers to join our growing team. We have openings across all sections and subjects, from Nursery to Class 8.

Pre-Primary Teachers

Nursery, LKG & UKG — early childhood educators with patience and creativity

Primary Teachers

Classes 1 to 5 — all subjects including English, Hindi, Maths, EVS & Science

Middle School Teachers

Classes 6 to 8 — English, Hindi, Mathematics, Science, Social Studies & Sanskrit

Special Subject Teachers

Computer Science, Art & Craft, Physical Education (PE/Sports), Music & Dance

Eligibility & Requirements

Qualification

B.Ed / D.El.Ed (NTT for Nursery) with a relevant graduate or postgraduate degree in the subject applied for

Experience

Freshers with strong subject knowledge are welcome; prior teaching experience is an added advantage

Skills

Good communication skills, classroom management, and a genuine passion for teaching young learners

Location

Candidates residing in or near Jaipur, Rajasthan, preferred for ease of commute
