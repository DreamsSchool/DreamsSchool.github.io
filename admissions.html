Admission Process

Thank you for your interest in Dream's School. We welcome applications from families who share our vision of providing quality education that develops well-rounded individuals. Our admission process is designed to be straightforward while ensuring the right fit for both the student and the school.

Admissions Open for Academic Year 2025-26

We are currently accepting applications for Classes Nursery to XII for the upcoming academic year.

Steps for Admission

1

Application Form

Collect and submit the application form along with the required documents. Application forms are available at the school reception or can be downloaded from our website.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students applying for Classes I and above will undergo an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their readiness for the grade they are applying to.

3

Parent & Student Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents will be invited for an interaction with the school management to understand mutual expectations.

4

Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will receive an admission offer letter. Admission is confirmed upon payment of the applicable fees within the specified timeframe.

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs of the student
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate from the previous school (for Classes II and above)
  • Report cards from the previous school (for Classes II and above)
  • Address proof (Aadhaar Card/Passport/Utility Bill)
  • Student's Aadhaar Card (photocopy)
  • Parents' ID proof (Aadhaar Card/PAN Card/Passport)
