Admission Process

1

Application Form

Collect and submit the application form along with required documents from the school reception or download from our website.

2

Entrance Assessment

Students applying for Classes I and above will undergo an age-appropriate assessment to evaluate their readiness.

3

Parent & Student Interview

Shortlisted candidates and their parents will be invited for an interaction with the school management.

4

Admission Confirmation

Selected candidates will receive an admission offer letter. Admission is confirmed upon payment of applicable fees.

Admission Requirements

Required Documents

  • Completed application form
  • Four recent passport-sized photographs
  • Birth certificate (original and photocopy)
  • Transfer Certificate from previous school (for Classes II and above)
  • Report cards from previous school
  • Address proof
  • Student's Aadhaar Card
  • Parents' ID proof

Age Criteria (as of April 1, 2025)

Nursery: 3+ years
LKG: 4+ years
UKG: 5+ years
Class I: 6+ years
Higher Classes: Corresponding age progression
