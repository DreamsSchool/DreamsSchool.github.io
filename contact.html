Frequently Asked Questions

What are the school timings?
The school operates from 8:00 AM to 2:30 PM for all classes from Monday to Friday. On Saturdays, the timings are from 8:00 AM to 12:30 PM.
Do you provide transportation services?
Yes, we provide transportation services covering various routes across Jaipur. The school buses are equipped with GPS tracking and are accompanied by trained attendants.
When do admissions open for the next academic year?
Admissions for the next academic year typically open in October. However, mid-term admissions are subject to availability of seats.
What curriculum does the school follow?
Dream's School follows the CBSE curriculum with an emphasis on holistic development through a blend of academic and co-curricular activities.
