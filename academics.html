Our Curriculum

Kindergarten Section (Class LKG)

Play-based learning focused on developing motor skills, language fundamentals, and social interaction through creative activities and sensory exploration.

Kindergarten Section (Class HKG)

Progressive curriculum introducing pre-reading, pre-writing, and basic numeracy skills, complemented by environmental awareness activities and artistic expression.

Kindergarten Section (Class Nursery)

Nurturing environment for first-time learners focusing on sensory development, basic communication skills, and social adjustment through guided play and interactive activities.

Kindergarten Section (Class Prep)

Structured program focusing on reading readiness, writing skills, and mathematical concepts, designed to build confidence and ensure smooth transition to primary education.

Primary Section (Classes I-V)

Focus on building strong foundations in language, mathematics, and environmental studies through activity-based learning.

Secondary Section (Classes VI-VIII)

Expanded curriculum with science, social studies, and languages enhanced by project-based learning initiatives.
